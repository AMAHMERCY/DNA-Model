About Our Solution

Missed appointments are a growing challenge in healthcare. They create delays, increase costs, and make it harder for patients to receive the care they need. We wanted to rethink this problem in a more intelligent way.

Our system uses predictive modelling to identify patients who are likely to miss their appointments. By analysing patterns in past attendance, reminders, demographics, and appointment history, the platform learns to spot risks early.

Once a patient is flagged as high-risk, the system can trigger tailored interventions, such as reminders, follow-ups, or flexible scheduling options. The aim is simple: reduce Did Not Attend (DNA) rates while improving patient engagement and making the booking process smoother for everyone.

It's a quiet blend of AI and practical design, built to support healthcare teams and make outpatient services more efficient.
